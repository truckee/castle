Occasionally asked questions
Question Answer
Where is Castle Rock HOA? Castle Rock HOA is a neighborhood of 182 homes on the southern face of Peavine Mountain in Reno, Nevada.The elevation here is about 5,400' above sea level. We're on the northern border of the Sierra Vista Park, a former golf course now serving as a bicycling park for all of Reno. Many homes here have fantastic views of the Mount Rose Wilderness and the Crystal Range mountains in California
When is Board election? Elections are held in May at the annual meeting.
When does the Board meet? The Board meets four times a year plus a budget ratification meeting in November.
